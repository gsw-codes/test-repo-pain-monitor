Debbie

Managing pain can be hard. Debbie is here to help you!
Available on Google Assistant and Alexa.
"Hey Google, get help from Debbie"

Debbie had a successful career in IT for many year before her chronic arthritis became too much.
Having struggled to find any specialist who could help,
a friend came across a cognitive pain management course and paper toolset, and the idea for a pain management app was born.
Build your confidence day by day
"Talk to Debbie" with Google Assistant or Alexa. Its as simple as that. You can then plan your goals, record pain, work through exercises or set reminders.
Set voice activated reminders. You can set them each day or each week at a time that suits you.
